Creamy Mushroom Risotto

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
  • 1 quart chicken or vegetable broth, warmed
  • 1 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 lb mushrooms, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, saut√©ing until they are translucent, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the rice and stir to coat with the butter. Cook for 2 minutes, then pour in the wine and cook until the wine is fully absorbed.
  3. Add the mushrooms and another tablespoon of butter. Cook until the mushrooms are soft, about 5 minutes.
  4. Begin adding the warmed broth one ladle at a time, allowing the rice to absorb the liquid before adding more. Stir frequently, and continue to add broth until the rice is tender and creamy, about 18-20 minutes.
  5. Stir in the remaining butter, Parmesan cheese, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
