Chicken Parmesan

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, for dredging
  • 3 large eggs
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1 cup fine dry bread crumbs
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan, plus extra for serving
  • Unsalted butter
  • Olive oil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh mozzarella, cut into small cubes
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Dredge each breast in the flour and tap off excess, then dip in the egg and let excess drip off, then dredge on both sides in the bread crumbs.
  3. In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of butter and 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook for about 5 minutes on each side until they are browned and cooked through. Remove the chicken and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the tomato sauce and bring to a simmer. Add the fried chicken back to the skillet and spoon some sauce over them.
  5. Sprinkle the chicken with the mozzarella, Parmesan, and basil. Bake in the oven until the cheese is bubbly, about 10 minutes.
  6. Remove from the oven and garnish with parsley and extra Parmesan. Serve hot.
